You are invited to take part in a research study about individual and group decision-making. Your participation will help us better understand how people interact with group members and authorities in various situations.

Your participation is entirely voluntary. If you agree to participate, you will receive a fixed fee of {{ session.config.participation_fee }}, as well as a bonus depending on your decisions and those of the other participants. The experiment will last approximately one hour.

There are no foreseeable risks associated with participating in this study. You are free to withdraw at any time, for any reason, without any penalty. However, since this is a group experiment, please note that your withdrawal could affect the participation of the other members of your group.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

All data collected in this study will be processed exclusively for scientific purposes. Your responses will remain completely anonymous and confidential. No personally identifiable information will be collected or linked to your decisions in the experiment. There will be no data transfer outside the European Union.

The data controller is Dimitri Dubois (CEE-M, CNRS, University of Montpellier, France). Data recipients are: Rustam Romaniuc (MBS Montpellier Business School), Dimitri Dubois (CNRS, University of Montpellier) and Andrea Guido (PSB Paris School of Business).

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data.
To exercise these rights, you can contact Dimitri Dubois at: dimitri.dubois@umontpellier.fr.

This study is funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR), project number ANR-21-CE26-0016. For any questions about the study, please contact Rustam Romaniuc at r.romaniuc@mbs-education.com.
