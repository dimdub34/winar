You are invited to take part in a research study about individual and group decision-making. Your participation will help us better understand how people interact with group members and authorities in various situations.

Your participation is entirely voluntary. If you agree to participate, you will receive a fixed fee of {{ session.config.participation_fee }} plus a bonus depending on your decisions and the decisions of other participants. The experiment will take approximately one hour of your time.

There are no foreseeable risks associated with participating in this study. You are free to withdraw at any time, for any reason, without penalty. Please note, however, that since this is a group experiment, your withdrawal may affect the participation of other group members.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

All data collected in this study will be processed strictly for scientific purposes. Your responses will remain completely anonymous and confidential. No personally identifiable information will be collected or linked to your decisions in the experiment. There will be no data transfer outside the European Union.

The data controller is Dimitri Dubois (CEE-M, CNRS, University of Montpellier, France). Data recipients include: Rustam Romaniuc (MBS Montpellier Business School), Dimitri Dubois (CNRS, University of Montpellier) and Andrea Guido (PSB Paris School of Business).

You have the right to access your data, to request correction or deletion of your data, to restrict or object to the processing of your data, and to request data portability.
To exercise these rights, you can contact Dimitri Dubois at: dimitri.dubois@umontpellier.fr.

This study is funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR), grant number ANR-21-CE26-0016. For any questions about the study, please contact Rustam Romaniuc at r.romaniuc@mbs-education.com.
